Let's face it, life can suck pretty hard sometimes

Thanks to the internet, we can now share how much our life sucks, and all understand each other better - which is great! It's just, sometimes it can feel like we’re being overwhelmed with negativity.

Yes, life is hard: but it's also beautiful, funny, inspiring, empowering...

At YourHappyPlace.co, we're not trying to ignore the bad bits, we’re just offering you a brighter perspective. We want to give our readers the chance to take some time out of their day to smile, maybe laugh and know that they're not alone in this crazy endeavour we call living.
